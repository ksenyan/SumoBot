This is the skateboard bot built by Ksenya and Ammarah!

It is controlled using an ESP32 and the Dabble app through Bluetooth. 


Parts:

2 TT DC Gear Motors

1 Servo Motor

4 Wheels (see stl files)

1 H-bridge

1 Node MCU Esp32

Lots of wires

3D-printed robot base and skateboard deck (see stl files)

1 9V Battery
